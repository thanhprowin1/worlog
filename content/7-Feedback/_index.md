> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. The members of the FCJ team are always ready to support when I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me focus better. However, I think adding a few social events or team-building sessions would help everyone understand each other better.

**2. Team Admin**  
 The team admin supported me with procedures, documents, and created favorable conditions for my work. I highly appreciate that the mentor allowed me to try and solve problems on my own instead of just giving me the answers.

**3. Relevance between Work and Major**  
The assigned tasks were highly relevant to the Software Engineering knowledge I acquired at school, while also opening up new areas that I had not previously approached. As a result, I was able to consolidate my foundational knowledge and learn practical skills simultaneously.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills, such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The mentors also shared practical experiences that helped me better orient my career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously, but maintains a fun atmosphere. During urgent projects, everyone tries their best and supports each other regardless of their position. This made me feel like an integral part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The company provides internship allowances and offers flexible working hours when necessary. In addition, participating in internal training sessions is a massive plus.

### Other Questions
- **What were you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
  The enthusiastic support from the mentors and the opportunity to directly participate in developing a real-world project (the Wakan Project) using cutting-edge technologies like Cloud Architecture and Serverless, rather than just doing minor tasks.
- **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**  
  Providing more detailed technical guidelines or a clearer initial orientation for those newly introduced to Cloud and AI would be great. This would help reduce the learning curve and confusion during the first week.
- **If introducing it to friends, would you recommend they intern here? Why?**  
  Absolutely. It is a highly professional and dynamic environment at AWS Vietnam that helps students rapidly improve both their problem-solving mindset and specialized skills.

### Proposals & Desires
- **Do you have any proposals to improve the internship experience?**  
  I suggest organizing weekly internal Tech Talks or practical experience-sharing sessions from Senior engineers to give interns deeper insights into real-world case studies.
- **Do you want to continue this program in the future?**  
  Yes, I strongly desire to continue working with the team in the upcoming phases of the Wakan project or to become a full-time employee if the opportunity arises.
- **Other feedback (free sharing):**  
  Thank you to the FCJ team and the company for creating such a fantastic playground and learning environment for a final-year student like me.
